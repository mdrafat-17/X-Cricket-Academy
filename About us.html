Founded in 2022, X Cricket Academy has become a trusted name in cricket coaching. With a team of experienced coaches, modern training techniques, and structured programs, we ensure that each player receives the best possible training. At X Cricket Academy, our mission is to inspire and train the next generation of cricketers. We believe that cricket is more than just a game—it’s a sport that builds discipline, teamwork, and resilience. Our academy is dedicated to providing high-quality coaching, state-of-the-art training facilities, and a supportive environment where players of all ages and skill levels can grow and excel. We welcome beginners, intermediate, and advanced players. Whether you’re learning the basics or refining your technique, our customized training programs help you reach your full potential.


Our Goal is to:

  • Develop technical skills and game strategies
  • Enhance physical fitness and mental toughness
  • Instill sportsmanship, leadership, and teamwork
  • Prepare aspiring players for competitive and professional cricket

Our Achievements:

  • Trained over 200 students since our opening in 2022.
  • 10+ players selected for district-level teams.
  • Hosted our first inter-academy tournament in 2024.

Our Core Values:

  • Passion: We are passionate about cricket and inspiring others to love the game.
  • Dedication: We are committed to the growth and development of every player.
  • Respect: We promote respect on and off the field, for teammates, coaches, and opponents.
  • Integrity: We believe in honesty, fairness, and strong moral principles in sports.

Whether you’re playing for fun, aiming for club-level cricket, or dreaming of representing your country, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Join us and be part of a community that lives and breathes cricket!
